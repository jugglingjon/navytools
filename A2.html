Appendix II

THE METRIC SYSTEM AND EQUIVALENTS

LINEAR MEASUREMENT EQUIVALENTS

1 Centimeter = 10 Millimeters = 0.01 Meter = 0.394 Inch

1 Meter = 100 Centimeters = 1,000 Millimeters = 39.370 Inches

1 Kilometer = 1,000 Meters = 0.621 Mile

WEIGHTS EQUIVALENTS

1 Gram = 0.001 Kilogram = 1,000 Milligrams = 0.035 Ounce

1 Kilogram = 1,000 Grams = 2.2 Pounds

1 Metric Ton = 1,000 Kilograms = 1 Megagram = 1.1 Short Tons

LIQUID MEASUREMENT EQUIVALENTS

1 Milliliter = 0.001 Liter = 0.034 Fluid Ounce

1 Liter = 1,000 Milliliters = 33.814 Fluid Ounces

SQUARE MEASUREMENT EQUIVALENTS

1 Square Centimeter = 100 Square Millimeters = 0.155 Square Inch

1 Square Meter = 10,000 Square Centimeters = 10.764 Square Feet

1 Square Kilometer = 1,000,000 Square Meters = 0.386 Square Mile

CUBIC MEASUREMENT EQUIVALENTS

1 Cubic Centimeter = 1,000 Cubic Millimeters = 0.061 Cubic Inch

1 Cubic Meter = 1,000,000 Cubic Centimeters = 35.315 Cubic Feet

TEMPERATURE CONVERSION

5/9 (degrees Fahrenheit (°F) - 32) = degrees Celsius (°C)

212 °F = 100 °C

90 °F = 32.2 °C

32 °F = 0 °C

9/5 C° + 32 = F°
